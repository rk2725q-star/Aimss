How the RAG AI System Works
Once uploaded, your materials are indexed by the AI. When students ask questions in the AI chatbot, the system automatically retrieves relevant sections from your notes and uses them to generate accurate, grounded answers.
1
Upload — PDF, image, or text file with study material
2
Extract — AI reads text via PDF parser or OCR (for images & diagrams)
3
Index — Content split into searchable chunks, stored securely
4
Retrieve — Student asks a doubt → relevant notes sections are found automatically
5
Answer — AI generates a response grounded in your actual class notes 🎯
